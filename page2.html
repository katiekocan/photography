I took up photography as a hobby just this last year! Photography has always been something that had interested me, but it wasn't until I got a Canon EOS Rebel T7 camera for Christmas 2022 that I really started taking it seriously. Since then, I have taken photos for various groups and organizations, such as 1901 Productions, Blue Hour Studios, and Alpha Chi Omega. I have also done freelance work doing headshots and fun photoshoots. Prior to photography, I had a major focus on videography.
